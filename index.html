what's this?

I've been into narrative games lately, and it makes me want to write interactive fiction again.

But I keep coming up with these really big ideas that would probably take years to write, if I could even do it justice...

I also feel like I've forgotten how to make small games. I miss doing that. So I'm deciding that all these stories I want to tell, I'm just going to try telling them through very small games.

Inspired by

  • The Parable of the Pottery Class
  • droqever.com
  • night confessional, The Sun Will Blind My Eyes and the various wonderful short free IF games I've played online (these are only my recent plays!)
